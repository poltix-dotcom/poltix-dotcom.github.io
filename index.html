Our Story

We are young professionals with strong cumulative experience over consulting, technology and community-building. We at POLTIX are extremely passionate towards developmental politics and aspire to build a people-centric approach for their representatives.

Integrating technical expertise into a strong understanding of electoral campaigns and the grassroot problems, we make people understand how they drive democracy.

Our Services

Data Analytics

We deploy critical analyses of the political landscape predicting the interplay of many factors that drive decisions in the elections. Done through the implementation of modern algorithms

Digital footprint

We create and sustain the online presence of the politicians to establish clearer means of communication through various social media channels. Done through clear-cut content curation with advanced Social Media Marketing techniques.

Technology support

We perform tech-driven management actions to increase visibility and approachability of the people towards their representatives. Done through tools such as sentiment analysis and building websites and apps.

Field Research

We ideate and organize events and actions to understand the needs of the people better and analyze the bridge in planning and execution from the representatives. Done through Grassroot opinion surveys, rallies and volunteer mobilisation

Strategy

We reinvent campaigns and help rethink policy-making to discard the age-old ideas of garnering votes. Done through compelling campaign design and narratives and leadership positioning.
